  • Leirin Edwards

    Contemporary / Jazz / Ballet / Hip Hop

    Leirin’s Dance Journey:

    Leirin has been dancing since she was just 2 years old and has been passionate about all things dance ever since. She grew up in Okotoks, Alberta, training at Alberta Dance Academy before moving to High River as a high school student to compete with Dance Tech Inc.

    As a senior, she trained under exceptional artists and choreographers, including:

    • Amanda Messner
    • Josh McKinnon
    • Sho-tyme
    • Paul Otterbein

    She still considers them great mentors to this day.

    Leirin has competed across Alberta and British Columbia throughout her life in various dance styles, including:

    • Tap
    • Jazz
    • Ballet
    • Hip Hop
    • Lyrical
    • Contemporary
    • Pointe
    • Musical Theatre

    She has won both provincial and national titles in solo and group performances.

    Professional Training & Experience:

    Since graduating in 2019, Leirin has pursued further dance education, training in contemporary, jazz, and ballet under renowned artists such as:

    • Wojciech Mochniej
    • Marie Forcier
    • Michele Moss

    She has mastered skills in improvisation and the choreographic process.

    Additionally, she has trained with the Calgary-based hip hop company illFX Education, specializing in various urban dance styles, including:

    • Vogue
    • Waacking
    • House
    • Locking
    • Litefeet
    • Afro

    She has learned from respected dancers such as Ajay Musodi, Katherine Burrowes, and Edgar Gilbert-Reyes.

    Leirin’s love for dance, both on and off stage, remains a driving force in her journey. She is eager and excited to share her passion with the Pound It team and looks forward to being in the studio with everyone soon.

  • Masha Bezdietko

    Hip Hop / Dancehall / House

    I have been dancing since I was 4 years old. My mom introduced me to the dance community, and a few years later, I was cast in the street dance team New Flame. I was part of this team for 6 years (2017-2024). In 2021, we entered the Top 10 teams in Ukraine and won first place in the audience vote.

    With New Flame, I explored various dance styles, from dancehall, hip-hop, and breakdance to contemporary and ballet. Additionally, I performed many solo acts on stage, earning multiple awards.

    I started teaching at 13 as a dance mentor and masterclass instructor at CD Studio in Sumy, Ukraine. After Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine, I had to move to another city, but my passion for dance never faded. I continued training under a new coach, focusing on freestyle and house dance. In 2023, I moved to Canada, where I continue developing my skills and sharing my knowledge with students.

  • Hope Shayla

    Hip Hop

    Hope grew up on a farm in Clive, Alberta, where she developed a love for movement and expression. Dance became her passion at a young age, sparking a lifelong dedication to connecting with others through music.

    With over 15 years of experience, Hope is classically trained in more than eight dance disciplines but found her true calling in hip-hop and acrobatics. She has competed with elite competitive teams and performed as a backup dancer, bringing power and versatility to every stage.

    Committed to fostering an inclusive and inspiring environment, Hope believes in the transformative power of movement. When she’s not in the studio, you’ll find her studying at RDP, in the gym, or creating art in nature.
